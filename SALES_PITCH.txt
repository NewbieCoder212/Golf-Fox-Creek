FOX CREEK GOLF CLUB APP — SALES PITCH
========================================

HEADLINE
--------
Turn your club tournament into an event — live on the course, in members' pockets, and on the clubhouse TV.


30-SECOND ELEVATOR PITCH
------------------------
"We built a golf club app that makes member tournaments feel like the Ryder Cup — team vs. team, match points updating as play happens, and a live leaderboard on the clubhouse TV so everyone can follow the action without refreshing a spreadsheet.

Local businesses get real visibility through curated sponsor placements right where members are looking — on the scorecard, at The Turn, and on the big screen during events.

It's one platform: run the event, score from the phone, and let the whole club watch it unfold live."


2-MINUTE PITCH (DEMO / MEETING)
-------------------------------
THE PROBLEM
Club tournaments are fun, but the experience often falls flat. Standings live in a Google Sheet. Nobody knows who's winning until the banquet. Sponsors get a logo on a sign and little else. Members go home and the energy dies at the 18th green.

WHAT WE BUILT
A member-first golf app with tournament tools built in — not bolted on.

RYDER CUP-STYLE TEAM COMPETITION
Split the field into two sides. Pair 2v2 match groups across tee times. As scores come in hole by hole, match points update automatically — wins, losses, and ties tracked per match and rolled up to a team leaderboard. Members see it in the app; captains and committees see who's carrying the team. It's the drama of match play, without the manual math.

LIVE CLUBHOUSE TV DISPLAY
Open one secure link on any TV or tablet in the pro shop or grill. Standings update in real time — net and gross, plus match points when it's a team event. A "Live" indicator and QR code on screen let members jump from the big screen to the full tournament on their phone. No app install required for the display — just a browser and a link from the pro shop.

ADVERTISING THAT REACHES MEMBERS WHERE THEY ALREADY ARE
Local partners — pro shop, restaurant, real estate, auto dealer — get placement in high-attention moments: tournament scorecard headers, hole sponsors on casual rounds, The Turn prompt at hole 9, and sponsor slots on the TV leaderboard (header, sidebar, footer). Club management controls what's active from an admin dashboard. It's member-relevant advertising, not generic banner ads.

THE RESULT
More engagement during the event. More reason to stay in the clubhouse after the round. A new revenue line for the club from local sponsors. And a tournament experience members actually talk about.


FEATURE HIGHLIGHTS — RYDER CUP-STYLE LEADERBOARDS
-------------------------------------------------
- Two-team format with Side A vs. Side B
- 2v2 match groups across rounds and tee times
- Hole-by-hole match results → automatic match points
- Team standings: points, wins, and matches played
- Supports scramble, best ball, alternate shot, and singles formats
- Mobile leaderboard card on the home screen during events


FEATURE HIGHLIGHTS — LIVE CLUBHOUSE TV
--------------------------------------
- Dedicated full-screen display URL (secure token — rotatable by managers)
- Real-time updates as scores are entered on course
- Net / gross toggle for standings
- Match points board for team events
- Landscape-optimized layout for clubhouse TVs
- QR code on screen → members open the tournament on their phone
- "Live" status so everyone knows standings are current


FEATURE HIGHLIGHTS — LOCAL MEMBER ADVERTISING
---------------------------------------------
- Curated sponsor banners — club-controlled, not random ads
- Placements: tournament scorecard header, hole sponsor, The Turn (hole 9), TV leaderboard
- Sponsor name, image, message, and optional link
- Admin dashboard to add, edit, and activate placements
- Ideal for local businesses targeting members who are already on the course
